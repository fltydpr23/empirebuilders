What We Build

Specialist Services,
Uncompromised Quality.

Whether you're planning a ground-floor extension, a luxury kitchen remodel, or a complete bathroom transformation, Empire Builders delivers end-to-end project management with vetted local tradespeople on-site every single day — no gaps, no delays.

01

Single Storey Extensions

Gain the space your family needs without moving. Our single-storey extensions blend seamlessly with your existing home — matching brickwork, rooflines, and proportions to perfection.

  • Full planning permission guidance
  • Architect & structural engineer coordination
  • Matching materials sourced locally
  • Turnkey project management
02

Two Storey Conversions

Double your living space with a two-storey extension that adds real value and room to grow. We manage every phase from foundations to finishing — tradespeople on-site every working day.

  • Party wall agreements handled
  • Structural calculations included
  • Lantern & feature window options
  • Loft conversion add-ons available
03

Bespoke Kitchens

The kitchen is the heart of your home — we treat it that way. From knocking through walls to installing statement islands and underfloor heating, we create spaces you'll love for decades.

  • Wall removal & RSJ installation
  • Roof lantern & bi-fold door options
  • Bespoke cabinetry & fitting
  • Tiling, electrics & plumbing
04

Luxury Bathrooms

Transform your bathroom into a personal sanctuary. Walk-in rainfall showers, freestanding baths, floor-to-ceiling stone tiling — Kev's exceptional tiling and our expert team will exceed expectations.

  • Walk-in shower & wet room design
  • Expert tiling — large format & mosaic
  • Underfloor heating installation
  • Full strip-out & replumb
How It Works

A Process as
Refined as
the Result.

From your first call to walking through your transformed home, every step is transparent, stress-free, and personally overseen by Antony.

1

Free Consultation

We visit your home, listen carefully to your vision, and assess the space. No pressure, no obligation — just honest expert advice on what's possible and what it will cost.

2

Design & Planning

We work with architects and structural engineers to bring your plans to life. Planning applications, building regulations, and structural calculations — all handled on your behalf.

3

The Build

Work begins on time and continues every day until completion. Antony personally oversees each phase, coordinating specialist trades — no gaps, no delays, no surprises. Ever.

4

Finishing & Handover

Every detail is inspected before we hand over the keys. We only call the job done when you're completely satisfied — and we remain available long after completion.
